Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. For international orders, delivery times vary by destination but generally range from 7-14 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships, allowing you to monitor its progress in real-time.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Some items like software, opened media, and special order products may be excluded. Please see our full return policy for details.

To initiate a return, please visit our returns portal or contact our customer service team.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. During checkout, you'll see the available shipping options and estimated delivery dates for your location.

Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team.

For any delivery issues, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for business and educational purchases. Please contact our sales team at sales@technex.com with details about your order requirements to receive a customized quote.

We also offer dedicated account managers for corporate clients to streamline the ordering process.
